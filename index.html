FRAVS

Fixed-Resolution Adaptive Video System

Optimizing Video Streaming Quality Under Low Bandwidth Conditions with Static Resolution Maintenance

Revolutionary research combining H.265/H.266 codec technology with machine learning to maintain video quality without resolution degradation in bandwidth-constrained environments.

Research Overview

Traditional Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR) systems often compromise visual quality by reducing resolution during bandwidth constraints. Our research presents a novel approach that maintains constant resolution while optimizing other video parameters for superior user experience.

Research Problem

Current ABR systems prioritize bandwidth accommodation through resolution reduction, creating a trade-off between stream integrity and visual quality. This is particularly problematic for educational content, medical consultations, and professional presentations where visual consistency is crucial for effectiveness.

Our Solution

FRAVS (Fixed-Resolution Adaptive Video System) maintains constant resolution while dynamically optimizing bitrate, frame rate, and encoding parameters. Our system integrates H.265/H.266 codec technology with LSTM-based bandwidth prediction and intelligent quality adaptation algorithms.

Key Innovation

Our approach fundamentally challenges the assumption that resolution reduction is necessary for bandwidth adaptation. By optimizing non-spatial parameters and using advanced compression techniques, we achieve superior results while maintaining visual consistency.

Methodology

Our comprehensive approach integrates multiple advanced technologies for optimal video streaming performance

Content-Aware Encoding

H.265/HEVC encoding with multi-dimensional content analysis including spatial, temporal, and frequency domain assessment.

  • ROI detection
  • Dynamic CRF adjustment
  • Motion analysis

Bandwidth Prediction

LSTM-based predictor trained on network sequences for accurate bandwidth forecasting and proactive adaptation.

  • 10-timestep sequences
  • Confidence thresholds
  • Hysteresis logic

Quality Adaptation

Intelligent engine that selects optimal profiles while maintaining static resolution through non-spatial parameter optimization.

  • Buffer-aware decisions
  • Quality oscillation reduction
  • Multi-tier profiles

Stream Packaging

CMAF-based segmentation supporting both DASH and HLS formats for cross-platform compatibility.

  • Low-latency segments
  • Adaptive delivery
  • Real-time monitoring
